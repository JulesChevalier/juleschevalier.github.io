Interests

Research

I started my PhD in October 2013, after a Web Intelligence Master from Université Jean Monnet (Saint Etienne, France).

My thesis is focused on Reasoning over the Web of Data. This makes my work mainly related with Semantic Web, Big Data, Parallel and Distributed Architectures, and Cloud Computing.

Teaching

I am currently involved in courses at Web Intelligence Master, located at Université Jean Monnet. More exactly, I am in charge of the course Middleware Networks.

Project OpenCloudWare

The OpenCloudware project aims at building an open software engineering platform, for the collaborative development of distributed applications to be deployed on multiple Cloud infrastructures. OpenCloudware is funded by the French Fonds national pour la Société Numérique (FSN), where is was accepted in the Cloud number 1 call under the name "CloudForce", and is supported by Pôles Minalogic, Systematic and SCS
